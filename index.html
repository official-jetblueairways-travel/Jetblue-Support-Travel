1. Phone Support

Reach JetBlue's main customer service line at +1-888-829-1117 (also known as 1-800-JETBLUE or 1-800-538-2583). This line is available 24/7 for assistance with bookings, cancellations, refunds, and more.

Pro Tip: To speak with a live representative, press 2 for reservations or general information, then press 3 to speak with an agent. Alternatively, pressing 0 may also connect you directly to a live person.

For non-urgent inquiries or to provide detailed information, email JetBlue at DearJetBlue@jetblue.com. Include your full name, contact details, flight information, and a clear description of your issue. Response times may vary, so for urgent matters, consider other contact methods.
